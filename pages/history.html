🕐 Major Events Timeline

Day 1 — Canvas Launch
🌐 GeoR Goes Live
The world canvas opens. First 1,000 users from 34 countries rush to draw. Brazil draws a flag first. The center remains blank.
Day 1 — 6 hours later
⚔️ First Territory War
Brazilian and Japanese users clash over the center canvas. PixelKing_BR claims dominance. NeonTokyo responds with pixel art.
Day 3
🎉 1 Million Pixels Milestone
The canvas crosses 1 million drawn pixels. 12,441 users from 97 countries had drawn. Brazil wins Pixel Wars I.
Day 7
🎨 World Art Day Alpha
First global collaborative event. 8,212 users create cultural artwork. A Moroccan calligraphy piece earns 24,000 likes.
Day 14–21
⚔️ Pixel Wars II — Japan Victory
Japan organizes the first coordinated alliance strategy. They methodically reclaim the center in 6 days. NeonTokyo becomes a legend.
Day 30
🌍 100M Pixel Milestone
100 million pixels drawn. MoroccanBrush wins the First Global Challenge with Arabic geometric art. GeoR trends on X.
Day 40
💥 The Great Erasure
An anonymous coordinated erase attack wipes 3% of the canvas. The community rebuilds in 48 hours, stronger than before.
Day 47 — TODAY
⚔️ Pixel Wars III — LIVE
The largest war yet. 847 active fighters. Brazil leads. The center changes hands every 30 minutes. History is being made.
